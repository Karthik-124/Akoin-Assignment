## Overview
A responsive single-page website built for the Institute of Digital Risk (IDR), an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber and AI risk professionals. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript.Submitted as part of an internship assignment for AKOIN.

https://akoin-idr.netlify.app/

## Design Decisions
The IDR mark uses a hexagonal cube to suggest structure, resilience, and layered complexity ,qualities central to digital risk management. Orange signals urgency and precision against a dark ground, while the isometric cube faces use differential opacity to imply depth and hierarchy without visual noise. The wordmark pairs a bold geometric sans for "IDR" with a tracked small-caps secondary line, balancing authority with technical clarity. Both variants scale cleanly from favicon to full-width header.
