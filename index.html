IDR_002 / About

What is IDR?

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute built for the intersection of digital technology, cyber security, and high-consequence risk management.

We exist because the gap between academic knowledge and operational readiness in digital risk is widening fast. Organisations operating in regulated environments face threats that academic programmes alone cannot fully prepare practitioners to address.

IDR bridges this gap. Through a unique partnership with a leading UK university, we combine intellectual rigour with real-world projects, regulatory frameworks, and live industry engagement — producing practitioners who are deployment-ready from day one.
